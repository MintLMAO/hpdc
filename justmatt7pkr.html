JustMatt7's Parkour, by JustMatt7

JustMatt7's parkour is rather unforgiving, especially since it gets harder the further you go- and there's ZERO checkpoints. This map, if excluding the last section, would be placed MUCH lower on the chart. However, due to the excessive series of jumps which could be considered savage in difficulty, the map must be rated at least peak savage due to its length. Additionally, there are red balls that are difficult to predict and deal with, especially when you must grab onto a part to progress (since the balls could interfere.) These balls spike the difficulty all the way up to mid insane. In conclusion this parkour is extremely difficult.

Difficulty: Mid Insane
